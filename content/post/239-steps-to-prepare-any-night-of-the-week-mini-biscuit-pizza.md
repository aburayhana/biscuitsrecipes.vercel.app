##### The ingredients needed to make Mini Biscuit Pizza:

1. Get 1/2 cup Sev
1. Take 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
1. Take as needed Tomato sauce
1. Take 2 tsp Chat masala
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Take as needed Mint leaves for garnish
